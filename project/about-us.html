About Us

Cherokee Run Club was established to unite people who are passionate about running and dedicated to maintaining a healthy lifestyle. Our club is not just a collection of runners; it is a vibrant community where friendships are formed, goals are met, and personal milestones are celebrated. We believe running has the power to change lives, build resilience, and create a strong sense of community.

Our Mission

Our mission is to inspire, support, and grow the running community in Cherokee County. We aim to provide a welcoming and inclusive environment where runners of all levels can thrive. Whether you're training for your first 5K or your next marathon, our club offers the resources, encouragement, and camaraderie you need to succeed.

Get Involved

There are many ways to get involved with Cherokee Run Club. Participate in our events, volunteer to help with races, or simply come out and cheer for your fellow runners. Your involvement helps strengthen our community and supports our mission of promoting a healthy and active lifestyle.

Join Us

We invite you to join Cherokee Run Club and become a part of our dynamic running community. Membership is open to runners of all ages and abilities. By joining, you’ll gain access to exclusive club events, training sessions, and a network of supportive and enthusiastic runners.
